EXCELLENCE IN AESTHETIC EDUCATION

International Academy for Cosmetology and Aesthetic Science (IACAS), formerly known as IAAS, is a pioneering institute in Aesthetic Studies in the Philippines. The academy takes the lead in professionalizing Medical Aesthetic and Cosmetology practice, through a combination of curriculum-based and hands-on training provided by experienced Doctors, Nurses, and Field Practitioners. They are the forefront of Aesthetic and Cosmetology Clinical practice in the country.
